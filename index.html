Team 7 in "Naruto" consists of Naruto Uzumaki, an energetic ninja with a dream to be Hokage; Sakura Haruno, a young woman who becomes a talented medical ninja; Sasuke Uchiha, a quiet and skilled ninja seeking vengeance for his clan; and their mentor, Kakashi Hatake, a highly respected ninja with a laid-back demeanor. Their shared journey forms the core of the series.
